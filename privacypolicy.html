General Data Collection

Our Discord Bots store certain Channel IDs, Server IDs, User IDs, Message IDs and possibly names in order for most of it's functions and features to work. These values are only accessed and used by the bot itself and not accessed by our team unless needed or unless given permission to. The values specified are publicly available and are not sensitive information or private credentials.

We do not directly store message content, we forward it to the different channels when needed. (Example: our modmail system) To opt out, simply disable the features! (Some features are automatically turned off) By running the dedicated cmmands to disable these features, all data associated with it, will be deleted. We will also not be able to recover that information, when deleted. If you are planning on disabling features, remember you will lose all your data!
